Galaxy Match - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2025

Overview

Galaxy Match ("the App") is a match-3 puzzle game developed for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

Galaxy Match does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No account creation is required
  • No personal information is collected (name, email, location, etc.)
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No advertising SDKs are included
  • No data is sent to any server

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only using Android DataStore:

  • Game progress (level scores, star ratings, unlocked levels)
  • Settings preferences (sound, haptic feedback, accessibility options)
  • Achievement progress
  • Daily challenge streaks and statistics

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone else. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or using the "Reset Progress" option in the app's Settings screen.

Internet Access

Galaxy Match does not require or use an internet connection. The App functions entirely offline.

Permissions

The App requests only the following permission:

  • VIBRATE: Used for haptic feedback during gameplay (can be disabled in Settings)

Children's Privacy

Galaxy Match does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

Galaxy Match does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
